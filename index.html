Places to visit

Touchwood

Solihull's award winning indoor shopping center.
Touchwood boasts a wide variety of
restaurants and high-end shops. You can also catch
a film at Touchwood's 9-screen Cineworld to break up your day.

The Core Library

Solihull's main library has fun for the whole
family. Home to an impressive collection of books and
an interactive children's section alongside a theatre,
cafe and art collection. You will always find something to do!
